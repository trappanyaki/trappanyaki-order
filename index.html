    FAQ

    Can I cancel my order?

    No refunds, but we'll happily reschedule you to the next available cook window — just DM us.

    Do you accommodate allergies or substitutions?

    Yes — let us know when you order and we'll do our best to accommodate allergy requests or ingredient swaps.

    Can I change my order after I pay?

    Once your order is confirmed, it's locked in for that cook window — no changes after that, so double-check before you send payment.

    How far ahead do I need to order?

    For same-day pickup, get your order in before 11 PM. After that, you're looking at the next available batch.
